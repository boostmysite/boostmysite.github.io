Software Installation

Step 1: Run EXCHANGER.EXE (for Windows) or EXCHANGER.APP (for Mac OS) or EXCHANGER.DEB (for Linux) and follow the ongoing steps by clicking on the next button.

Step 2: Click on the ADD NEW SESSION button in the traffic exchange page to create your unique session key.

Step 3: Enter this UNIQUE SESSION KEY to start your session.
