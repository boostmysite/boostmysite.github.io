Customizing your Settings

You can update your username and email address or change your password in the SETTINGS tab. You can also access your affiliate settings, billing addresses and saved cards from this tab.

AFFILIATE SETTINGS include all of your personal information such as personal address as well as Paypal and Payoneer accounts.
